Here are our 3 top picks of well rounded excersises to get you going!

Excersise number one is pushups. Pushups is a movement where you position yourself horizontaly on the ground, with your arms pushed to the ground in line with your shoulders. As seen in the video you then push yourself up and down to create tension in the breast and back area as well as the triceps.

The second excersise is squats. Squats is a movement where you as it says, squat down and keep your backstraight as well as pushing yourself up and down. This targets the whole lower body area as shown in the video.

Our last excersise we provide free to you is situps. Situps is an excersise where you lay down on your back with your knees up in the air 90 degrees. You then tighten your stomach and pull yourself up towards your knees. This excersise will strengthen your core and stomach muscles as shown in the video.
